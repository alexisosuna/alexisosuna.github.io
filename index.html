Projects

Solar Eclipse Telescope

In the Summer of 2016, I was handed a Solar Telescope and instructed to learn how to use it. I had no expertise or experience with astrology or telescopes, and I was tasked to learn how to successfully operate it with absolutely no help. In a matter of weeks, I was able to complete a step-by-step tutorial for the most novice of users and became the designated technician for the telescope during the 2017 Solar Eclipse.


Solar Water Heater

While attending Heritage University, the Pre-Engineering program offered a specialty course instructed by Noble Peace Prize Recipient Michael Durst (shown below), who trained our class how to install and operate a solar hot water heater. Upon the successful installations in two local homes, we were certified to install solar hot water heaters under private contractors.
